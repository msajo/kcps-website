Activaties

Camp
Camping with KCPS is fun.

At KCPS every year starting from Grade 3 we go camping with our friends for 3 days. This is so our students can get good life experience.This also lets you make lots of fun memories of being at school.

Class party
We have a party at the end of every term.

At KCPS at the end of each term all classes will have a party to celebrate their accomplishments this term and to have fun.We also have so that we can spend bonding together as a class and get to know each other a bit better.

Cross Country
Cross country is a really good tradition at KCPS.

AT KCPS we always have a cross country race every year to get some physical excersie and to work as a team.we also have it so that we can take a break from our learning and enjoy ourselves.
